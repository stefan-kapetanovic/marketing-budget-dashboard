UK Visa Requirements & Digital Transition

Step-by-step clearance guidelines specifically for Serbian citizens.

Crucial Rule for Serbian Travelers

The new UK **ETA (Electronic Travel Authorisation)** only applies to countries whose citizens can travel visa-free. As a Serbian passport holder, **you still need to apply for the Standard Visitor Visa** before departing.

Process Timeline:

  1. 1
    Online Application (gov.uk)

    Submit comprehensive details on salary, ties, and agenda. Payment of **£135.00** visa fee is finalized during submission.

  2. 2
    Self-Upload Supporting Docs

    All assets must be translated to English by a court interpreter. Upload digitized pay-slips, bank records, and booking plans directly on the VFS Global web portal.

  3. 3
    Biometrics Appointment (Belgrade)

    Visit VFS Global in New Belgrade to register fingerprints and photos. **Your physical passport is handed back to you immediately.**

  4. ✓
    The "eVisa" Transition

    Physical sticker vignettes are eliminated. Your status is stored entirely digitally. Link your visa directly to your passport by setting up an official **UKVI Account** online.

MVNOs do not own physical infrastructure, but they run silently on the same towers as the Big Four. You can easily pick these up inside local grocery supermarkets (Tesco, Sainsbury's) or off-licenses for £1.00, then purchase packages online.
